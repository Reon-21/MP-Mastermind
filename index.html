How to play

Goal: Guess the hidden pattern, in both order and color

Insert your guess into the empty sockets

Hints are provided on the right side in black and white pegs

Black pegs corresponds to one peg form your guess is both right color and right position

White pegs corresponds to one peg form your guess is the right color but wrong position

The position of black and white pegs does not correspond to the position of the guess
