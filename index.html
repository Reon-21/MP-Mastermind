Mastermind Game

How to play

Goal: Guess the hidden pattern, in both order and color

1.Insert your guess into the empty sockets

2.Hints are provided on the right side in black and white pegs

3.Black pegs corresponds to one peg from your guess is both right color AND right position

4.White pegs corresponds to one peg from your guess is the right color BUT wrong position

5.The position of black and white pegs does not correspond to the position of the guess
